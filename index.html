Questions \u2014 03
\n

Before you ask.

\n
\n\n
\n How long does a project take?+\n

A Sprint runs about two weeks; a full Build, around four. I take on a limited number of projects at once so yours never sits in a queue.

\n
\n
\n Do you work with both freelancers and companies?+\n

Both, often in the same month. Solo founders get the same process as a ten-person marketing team \u2014 the scope flexes, the standard doesn't.

\n
\n
\n What do you need from me to start?+\n

A short call and whatever you already have \u2014 brand assets, rough copy, a competitor you admire. If you have none of that, we figure it out together in week one.

\n
\n
\n Can you write the copy too?+\n

Yes \u2014 and I'd encourage it. The words and the design are the same decision made twice. Copy direction is included in every engagement.

\n
\n
\n Do you build the site or just design it?+\n

Both. Designs ship as a live, responsive site on the platform that fits you \u2014 no handing off a flat file and wishing you luck.

\n
\n
\n What if I only need a refresh?+\n

A Sprint is often exactly that \u2014 one page reworked to do the heavy lifting. Tell me what's underperforming and we'll start there.
